## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-27

### Added

- Initial local-first Streamlit simulator.
- Independent educational physics engine.
- Performance curves, design score, recommendations, and CSV/JSON exports.
- Competition generator and resistive-load model.
- Electrical score in mW, voltage, current, and trial energy.
- Electrical-power performance curve.
- Editable multi-section blade geometry table with a 50 cm competition preset.
- Blade cutting preview and chord/twist distribution charts.
- Classroom airfoil families with simplified lift/drag, Reynolds number, stall warning,
  and mW impact.
- NACA section airfoils, station-level airfoil roles, and airfoil profile preview charts.
- BEMT-lite section-force model for classroom blade comparisons.
- Blade physical effects for mass, rotor inertia, spin-up, startup torque, and surface finish.
- Design comparison table for saving candidate runs in the local session.
- Printable Markdown design sheet export.
- English dashboard guide and key-term glossary.
- Local installation guide in README, dashboard Guide, PDF manual, and docs.
- Streamlit viewer toolbar mode to hide local Deploy controls from students.
- Unit tests and project documentation.
